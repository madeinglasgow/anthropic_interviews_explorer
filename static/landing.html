What This Explorer Adds

The original Anthropic dataset contains raw interview transcripts. This explorer enhances the data with AI-extracted metadata for each interview, including:

  • Job title and industry of the participant
  • Experience level (entry-level, mid-career, senior, executive)
  • Overall sentiment toward AI tools
  • AI tools mentioned during the interview
  • Primary use cases for AI in their work
  • Key pain points and challenges with AI
  • Project summaries describing recent AI-assisted work

These extracted fields are normalized into standard categories, enabling aggregated analysis and visualization across all 1,250 interviews.

Key Findings

General Workforce

  • 86% reported time savings
  • 65% expressed satisfaction with AI's workplace role
  • 69% mentioned social stigma around AI use

Creatives

  • 97% reported time savings
  • 68% noted quality improvements
  • 70% managed peer judgment concerns

Scientists

  • 91% wanted more AI assistance
  • 79% cited reliability concerns
  • Use confined to writing and coding tasks
